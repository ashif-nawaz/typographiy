About us

Olgok

Olgok Guest House is at the heart of the exquisite picturesque Hunder Hamlet which is home to the famous double hump Bactrian camel. With a total of 6 rooms with attached modern bathrooms and 24x7 Hot & cold water, enjoy the exclusivity on this sprawling 2 acres grassy land with organic Kitchen garden, numerous apple & apricot trees and varieties of followers all around.
